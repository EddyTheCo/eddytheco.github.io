Criticality and phase diagram study of the long-range quantum Ising chain

by Eduardo Gonzalez Lazo on Thursday, March 11, 2021



This talk was given in a mini-meeting of my group at ICTP, Trieste, in online form due to Covid. The presentation is about calculations of the critical properties and phases of the quantum Ising chain with power-law decaying interactions. Here I introduce the model, give an introduction about the previous studies in these systems, the importance of these systems and how they can be studied. Then, the methods used by us to characterize the critical behavior are explained. In the end the results obtained and some conclusions are presented
